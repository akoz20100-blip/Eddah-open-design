حي لبن فقط

Language:
Arabic RTL

CTA direction:
WhatsApp-first

“فنيك في حيك، تواصل وأبشر بالسعد”

“خدمة صيانة منزلية منظمة وسريعة في حي لبن لخدمات السباكة والكهرباء والتكييف، بمواعيد واضحة وضمان على الشغل.”

Design direction

The site should feel:

* light
* warm
* premium
* local Saudi
* modern
* organized
* trustworthy
* not generic
* not dark
* not basic

Use the Orbai reference only as a quality benchmark:
https://orbai-template.framer.website/

Do not copy Orbai.

Aim for:

* premium Framer-like composition
* layered cards
* stronger image placement
* smooth motion
* high mobile polish
* advanced spacing and hierarchy

Key sections

The final landing page should include:

1. Navbar
2. Hero
3. Services
4. How it works
5. حي لبن map/local section
6. Why EDDAH
7. Tools / precision / visual trust section
8. Testimonials or honest sample reviews
9. Final CTA
10. Footer

Most important UI issue to avoid

Do not make the services section a basic 3-card grid.

Instead use:

* layered editorial cards
* overlapping panels
* floating badges
* premium crops
* glass/stone surfaces
* subtle hover depth
* map-line accents
* mobile-friendly visual rhythm

* confirm /brand/laban-map.png is used
